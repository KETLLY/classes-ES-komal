Cómo funciona cada clase

Este es el formato standard que vamos a usar en every single class, 50 minutos.

Standard 50-Minute Lesson Format

5 min
Opening / AperturaShort check-in en español + warm-up question sobre Bogotá, work, o travel.
10 min
Guided InputLa profesora presenta un place/situation/short text y introduce key vocabulary en context.
20 min
Controlled PracticeRoleplays, Q&A, repetition de useful chunks, selective correction.
10 min
Free ProductionKomal talks about her experience, plans, o opinions connected al theme.
5 min
Closure & PlanningSummary de easy/difficult points + next theme + optional small task.

How each class is structured

  • Every week covers one theme, split across 2 classes.
  • Each class has su own learning outcome (lo que Komal will be able to do al terminar).
  • Each class lists el specific language content (grammar, vocabulary, functional phrases).
  • Progress metrics se revisan en Weeks 4, 8, y 12 (checkpoints).
